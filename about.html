about.

I am currently studying a Bachelor Degree of Information Science in Digital Economy at KMUTNB.

My journey into web development is anything but ordinary. Once on the path of medical education, the disruptions of COVID-19 and the 2021 political changes became my unexpected launchpad into tech. I decided to embrace a minimalist approach to learning—focusing on what truly matters and cutting out the noise.

Starting with graphic design courses on LinkedIn Learning and Skillshare, with the purpose of transition into frontend development, picking up HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and styling frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind. My curiosity didn't stop there—I added Python to my toolkit and am now exploring React.js, PHP, and advanced programming technologies.

When I'm not coding or studying, I balance my life with gaming sessions that challenge my problem-solving skills, cooking, and regular workouts that keep me physically and mentally sharp—all approached with a minimalistic mindset of intentionality and purpose.

My transition from medical education to tech isn't just a career change—it's a testament to adaptability, continuous learning, and the power of pursuing passion with clarity and focus, regardless of the circumstances.
